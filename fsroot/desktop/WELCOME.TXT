Welcome to your PumpkinOS desktop folder.

The graphical desktop lets you browse the filesystem: double-click a
folder to open it, use the ".." icon (top-left) to go back up, and click
a file to see its details. The path you're in is shown on the taskbar,
next to the Start button.

Drop your own files in here - anything you put in /desktop shows up as an
icon when you open this folder.
